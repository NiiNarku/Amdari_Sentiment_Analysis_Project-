# Amdari_Sentiment_Analysis_Project-
## A comprehensive NLP pipeline designed to analyze customer sentiment for the ShopEase e-commerce platform.

The end of the supply chain is where promises meet reality. A delivery that arrives late, products that do not match expectations or a frustrating return process.  They are operational failures, and they show up in what customers say. This project listens to that analytically. Uisng BERT-based NLP, it analyses and predict customer reviews. 

## Key Objectives 
- A fully functional, end-to-end sentiment analysis system capable of processing and analyzing large volumes of customer feedback in real time.
- Automated identification of key drivers of customer satisfaction and dissatisfaction, enabling targeted improvements in products and services
- A scalable and multilingual system capable of adapting to growing data volumes and expanding market operations.
